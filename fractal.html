This online fractal viewer has a few of the features that professional "fractal creators" have, such as custom pallete options and fractal types! Currently, it lacks multithreading support. Click here to learn a little about it. Use the "More..." button to look at the options and shortcuts.
Anyways, hello!

RGB or hex values (for hex values, the # symbol is optional) should be separated by spaces. They should only contain 3 numbers (for example, black would be written as 0,0,0). The last color is the interior color. Maximum of 2,000 colors.
