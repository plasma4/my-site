Trees produce fruit, but planting one will cost 5 of that type of fruit and 1 water.

Fuji apples boost Gala fruit production by +10% per apple, Gala boosts Red Delicious, and so on.

Pipes produce water, while fertilizer improves Fuji apple gain.

(Currently increasing production by . Fertilizer costs 10.000B Gala apples and 1,000 water.)

Pesticides increase Fuji apple gain and Fuji tree gain by +100%, but the effect is capped at 99 pesticides. They cost 2.500q Red Delicious each!

Compostors take the fruit you give it and turn it into 5 Fujilizer per second! Each one costs 10.000S Honeycrisps.

Flowers also boost Fujilizer production. Each flower costs 100.00T Fuji apples, 1.000q Gala apples, 10.000q Red Delicious, and 100.00q Honeycrisps.

Mushrooms boost Fujilizer production by +1,000% for every one that you have. They cost 10.000N Honeycrisps. Note that this effect stacks with other boosts and items!
